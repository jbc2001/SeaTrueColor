About Sea True Color

Sea True Color is an open-source utility for recovering accurate color in underwater imagery.

Ocean water both scatters and absorbs light wavelengths, causing photos taken underwater to skew blue and lose vibrancy. This application compensates for those optical effects.

All processing is performed locally in your browser to protect your privacy.
